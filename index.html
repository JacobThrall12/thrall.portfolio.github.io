About Me

Hey there! I'm Jacob Thrall, and I'm currently a Junior studying Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile at Boise State University, which is part of the College of Innovation. My focus is on UI design, web development, and game development, with a particular interest in environmental building. I'm passionate about working with technology and pushing the boundaries of my creative potential. It's a thrill for me to use my skills to create engaging and interactive experiences that captivate users and leave a lasting impression.


In addition to my academic pursuits, I also have a few hobbies that I enjoy in my free time. I am an avid reader and enjoy getting lost in a good book. I also love hiking and exploring the great outdoors, taking in all the natural beauty that surrounds me. Lastly, I am a big football fan and enjoy watching games with friends and family, discussing the latest plays and predictions for the season. These hobbies help me recharge my creative batteries and keep me balanced amidst the demands of my studies and work.
